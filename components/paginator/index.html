Paginator

Allows users to navigate to next or previous page when content is split into several pages

Client-Facing Terminology

In client-facing documentation, refer to the action rather than the paginator.

  • Click 2 to move to the second page.
  • Click the arrow to move to the next page.

Guidance

The paginator can be used as navigation to break apart large quantities of content. It’s most common use case is at the bottom of a data table, but it can be used in other scenarios as well. 

Do not use the paginator component on pages that utilize infinite scroll, or when switching between slides or content in a carousel.

Best practices

  • Pagination should be right aligned under category or search results content. (Still to be determined).
  • Always place the pagination at the bottom of the content it’s paginating.
