Login

Make a good first impression! The login page is the first thing users see, in most cases. 

Guidance

A full login experience may consist of two parts:

1. Login experience (Required) Allows a user to access the app on day-to-day basis. 

2. Self register experience (Optional) Enables a user to create an account with the app. 

Apps may use Tyler ID (TID) to handle their login experience or they may design their own login to spec as needed.

Login

A login experience is made up of the following sections:

1. Client branding or TID logo (Required)

Backoffice apps use the default TID logo.

Citizen facing apps feature a client logo. The logo section should have a max height of 112px. Square or round logos should be 112px by 112px in dimension.  Rectangular logos should have an aspect ratio of 1:4 or 1:3.

2. Custom text (Required)

Backoffice apps use “Sign in with your Tyler account.” by default.

Citizen facing apps can feature custom client text, up to 80 characters (including spaces). “Sign in with your account” is provided by default.

3. Link to reset username (Optional)

If applicable, provide users with the ability to reset their own username or provide an option to recover a forgotten username if need be.

4. Show password (Optional)

Especially for mobile logins, allowing users to unmask their password can support memory and allow users to check their work. (Norman Nielsen, 2015).

5. Link to reset password (Required)

Provide users an option to reset or recover their password (ie, by sending a request to an admin).

6. Sign up / sign in options (Required)

Allows users to sign in to the app. If users can create their own accounts, provide access to register or sign up. Include an option to “Remember me,” if possible, which defaults in users’ credentials in the future.

7. Social logins (Optional)

Apps may provide links to appropriate social logins.

8. Additional custom text (Optional) 

Backoffice and citizen apps may use this area for additional custom client content or content such as links to privacy and security information.

9. Custom background image (Optional)

Citizen facing apps may select from provided full page templates or provide their own custom image.

Register

Registration allows users to sign up for an app. A registration process may include a signup form and email confirmation. 

1. Client branding or TID logo (Required)

Backoffice apps use the default TID logo.

Citizen facing apps feature a client logo. The logo section should have a max height of 112px. Square or round logos should be 112px by 112px in dimension.  Rectangular logos should have an aspect ratio of 1:4 or 1:3.

2. Custom text (Required)

Backoffice apps use “Sign up for an account.” by default.

Citizen facing apps can feature custom client text consisting of a headline and supporting text that may include sign up instructions or privacy policy information. The headline should not exceed 80 characters (including spaces) and the supporting text should not exceed 150 characters.

3. Social logins (Optional)

Apps may provide links to appropriate social logins.

4. Registration fields (Required)

Limit registration fields to the minimum of what’s needed to establish an account. 

5. Option to sign in with existing account (Required)

Provide users the option to sign in with an existing account in case they’ve reached the register page by mistake. 

6. Background (Required)

Registration forms should use the default Tyler grey background color. 

Best practices

Do.

Provide users with their password requirements up front.
