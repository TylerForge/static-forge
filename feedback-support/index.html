Feedback & support

Need help with content writing? Looking for a new component or illustration for your app? Have some feedback for our team? Reach out to us! Forge grows as teams contribute new solutions and pitch new problem spaces. 

 

Components

Think our components need a new feature? Request an enhancement to an existing component.

Found a bug? Let us know!

 

Need a new component, but don’t see it here? Create a ticket for our team to review the need.
What to include in your Jira ticket
  • The component name
  • Reason for the enhancement
  • Which teams (including yours) need the enhancement
What to include in your Jira ticket
  • Affected component name
  • Supporting screen recordings or screenshots if applicable
What to include in your Jira ticket
  • Any supporting images that you can provide
  • Which teams (including yours) will use this component
  • Whether your team has fully or partially adopted the Forge design system

Illustration and content

Need a new illustration, but don’t see it on our library? Create a ticket to request a new illustration.

Need some copy for your app? We can help you with your content needs.

 

Need an empty state or a page error pattern?

What to include in your Jira ticket
  • Whether it's a spot (small), spot-hero (medium), or hero (large) illustration
  • What the illustration should convey
  • Where the illustration will occur
What to include in your Jira ticket
  • Your product area (financials, asset management, utilities, courts & justice, etc)
  • The context in which the content will occur
  • Whether the content will be accompanied by an illustration
What to include in your Jira ticket
  • Your product area (financials, asset management, utilities, courts & justice, etc)
  • Whether you need an empty state or a new error pattern
  • The context in with the pattern will occur
