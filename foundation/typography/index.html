Typography

Tyler Forge provides a constrained, purposeful set of typographic styles based on the Material typography classes. 

Overview

Consistently tying typographic styles to appropriate sections and functions in an interface creates clear visual patterns and expectations for our products. 

This is Roboto, our Tyler Forge typeface.

Roboto features friendly and open curves and allows letters to be settled into their natural width. This makes for a natural reading rhythm more commonly found in humanist and serif types.

Guidance

Select from the following typography styles for your application. By default, type will inherit the body color Black at 87% opacity. For text that should have less visual emphasis, use Black at 54%.

Best practices

Do.

For desktop apps, use a max line-length of 75 characters to facilitate optimal reading patterns. Center your content or use columns to constrain content width. 

On mobile, use a max line-length of 35 characters. 

Don’t.

Avoid text that wraps the full width of a desktop app.
